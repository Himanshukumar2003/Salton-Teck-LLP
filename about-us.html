Salton Teck LLP

Salton Teck LLP, is a leading designer and manufacturer specializing in architectural, landscape, and decorative roadway luminaires, with a strong emphasis on advancing LED technology. Their diverse product range includes LED streetlights, floodlights, and smart lighting solutions designed for various applications such as outdoor lighting, architectural accents, façade illumination, and decorative street lighting. In addition to lighting, Salton Teck offers innovative smart water and energy meters, utilizing LoRaWAN technology for real-time monitoring and efficient resource management. Their commitment to quality and innovation has earned them national recognition and brand credibility, making them a trusted partner in transforming infrastructure with precision and reliability.
