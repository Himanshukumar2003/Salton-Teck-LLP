Nature Switch- CCMS

Salton Teck is the authorized channel partner of Kakatiya Energy Systems, offering the advanced Nature Switch – GSM EC series. This innovative solution provides automatic control of electrical loads with a remote control and monitoring interface. It operates using a unique infrared light sensor, absolute time settings, astronomically calculated sunrise and sunset times, or a combination of these methods, ensuring precise and efficient load management for various applications.

Features

  • Sensor Mode (Dusk to Dawn Operation)

  • Astronomical (Dynamically calculated sunset, sunrise times on daily basis)

  • Real Time based Mode( Single Cycle, Double Cycle, Triple Cycle in 24 hours)

Benefits

  • 100% Saving of manual Switching and monitoring cost

  • Elimination of all the energy wastage

  • Ensures definite and reliable switching at the desired time

  • Real time monitoring of current, power, energy and electrical abnormalities dance
